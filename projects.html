3D Renderer - updating

This is a cpu-based rasterizer write in C with sdl library to draw pixels on the screen. I've write basic libraries of matrix, vector, traingle, light, texture etc. just to better understand the render pipeline by myself. The renderer supports perspective, Orthographic, Isometric projection, view transformation, back-face culling, z-buffering, texture mapping and basic diffuse shading. I'll write a detailed article about how it works quickly once i found some time. Will keep update with here the article .

Voronoi Diagram Based City Generator: A Procedural Generation Approach to Create Adjustable City in Games

This project is my bachelor's thesis. It use a new approach to generating procedural cities for digital games and simulations using Voronoi diagrams, aiming to overcome the challenges of diversity, efficiency and control of urban environments. Developed in Unreal Engine 5 and implemented in C++, the project writes algorithms for generating urban components such as roads, terrain and buildings to enhance interactivity and realism. The system has a modular architecture with a user-friendly interface that allows developers to customize various city parameters. Details are here article .

Audio Visualizer

The audio visualizer I created used the Fast Fourier Transform to visualize audio by separating the audio into different frequencies, then map the magnitude of value into different mesh and shading attributes, eg. the shape of mesh, the color of light, the brightness of material etc. The audio visualizer is implemented in unity with C#.
